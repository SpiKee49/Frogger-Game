Goal of the game

Every level is based on score gained, in order to get to next level you need to score at least 400 points in current level except LEVEL 3(it's the last level). Main goal of the game is to get to the final level called SPACE and try to earn as much points as you possibly can.

  • LEVEL 1: avoid cars and get to the other side of the highway
  • LEVEL 2: jump on the logs in order to get to the other side don't get in contact with water get on the other shore
  • LEVEL 3: jump on the asteroid in order the get to the moon, when the player reaches you gain 100 score points
